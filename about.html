About the Studio e3gallery

A small studio in Barcelona. Twenty-two landscapes carried home from the road.

The Photographer
Reynisfjara · Iceland

A traveller with
a trained eye, betting
on calm and on light.

I make photographs of places that hold still long enough to be looked at. Iceland in the off season, Provence in the gap weeks, the Picos at first light — landscapes I returned to until they let me in.

The work is patient and quiet. No filters, no compositing, nothing invented. A long walk, the right hour, a tripod set on cold ground. What comes back from those mornings is what you see here.

The studio is in Barcelona. The road is everywhere else.

The Prints

Made small,

Each print is an archival pigment print on premium photo paper — Premium Luster for landscapes, Enhanced Matte for select verticals. Pigment ink, archival quality, made to last decades on a wall.
